MEMBERS AND AGENCY

SEVENTEEN is a powerhouse of 13 unique personalities that somehow fit together like the world’s most talented puzzle. Led by S.Coups, the group is split into three units—Hip-Hop, Vocal, and Performance—but they’re best known for being "self-producing idols" who craft their own music and high-energy choreography. Whether it’s Woozi locked in the studio, Hoshi quite literally "tiger-powering" through a dance routine, or the chaotic energy of the BSS trio (Seungkwan, DK, and Hoshi), they bring a mix of top-tier talent and genuine sibling-like chemistry. From the oldest members like Jeonghan and Joshua to the "maknae on top" Dino, they’ve managed to turn the math of "13 members + 3 units + 1 team" into a global phenomenon.

Pledis Entertainment adalah agensi hiburan asal Korea Selatan yang didirikan pada tahun 2007 oleh Han Sung-soo. Nama agensi ini terinspirasi dari gugus bintang Pleiades dalam rasi bintang Taurus, yang melambangkan ambisi untuk melahirkan bintang-bintang paling bersinar di industri musik.
